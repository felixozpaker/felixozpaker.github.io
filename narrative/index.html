My Morning...

Hello! I'm Felix! The following is a chronological showcase of my typical morning routine:









BED

Even though it's a school day and I want to stay asleep, since it's 6:30 AM, I have to wake up.

Right after waking up, my bed is all messy.

To start my day, I like to make my bed first.







BATHROOM

After making my bed, I immediately head to the shower. I have to shower in order to wake myself up and make me feel ready for the day.

After showering I brush my teeth. I brush for about 2 minutes every morning.

Once I've brushed my teeth, I pick out what clothes I am going to wear for the day.







BACKPACK/CLOTHES

Finally, I must get my backpack ready. I have to put water, my lunchbox, and sometimes a breakfast into it before I leave.

It was cold, so I put on a jacket.

Which shoes am I going to wear?







LEAVING

I chose my ugg slippers because it was cold outside and they keep my feet warm.

Leaving for school!

At last, I get in my car, ready to drive to school. The day has started.
